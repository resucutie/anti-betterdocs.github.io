BetterDocs & Discord Source are unsafe.

The creator of BetterDocs and Discord Source have been posting peoples themes and plugins on their websites and has been refusing to take them down. This is unfair to developers who dedicate time into their themes and plugins. For more info see the FAQ.

If you are a dev and want to protect your content, paste our BetterDocs Blocker at the top of your theme file. This should block all links to and from the site and invites to their server.

@import url('https://anti-betterdocs.github.io/BlockBetterDocs/code.css');
Don't want to use the blocker? Try including this license in your theme.
